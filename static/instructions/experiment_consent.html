Consent to participate in to-do list experiment

Welcome! This is a psychology experiment being conducted by Valkyrie Felso from the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems. In order to consent to participate, you MUST meet the following criteria:

  • You are currently a working professional.
  • 18 years of age or older.
  • Fluent speaker of English.
  • Have not participated in this experiment before.

This study is designed to investigate the effects of different types of digital to-do list tools on productivity and motivation.

The study will take about 45 minutes. You will sign up for our to-do list website, perform some usability tasks, and fill out several questionnaires. If you complete all parts of this HIT you will earn $6. If you complete only part of the study you will earn a base payment of $1.5 + the bonus(es) for each section you do complete.

Your participation in this research is voluntary. You may refrain from answering any questions that make you uncomfortable and may quit the study at any time without penalty.

Participating in this study might help you become more productive, procrastinate less, and be more satisfied with your daily accomplishments. The results may lead to the development of productivity tools that convey these benefits to everyone who wants to use them.

Information about how you use the to-do list tool will be logged and analyzed. However, your account on the to-do list website will be anonymous, and the tool will not be asking for any additional personally identifying information. Your responses will be handled confidentially, and nobody except for your will be able to see the names of the activities you put on your to-do lists. We cannot guarantee the confidentiality of information transmitted over the Internet. We will be keeping some data collected as part of this experiment indefinitely.

If you have any questions about the study, feel free to contact us at re-mturk@tuebingen.mpg.de.

By selecting the "consent" option below, I acknowledge that I am 18 or older, that I am a fluent speaker of English, that I have not completed this experiment before, that I have read this consent form, and that I agree to take part in the research.
